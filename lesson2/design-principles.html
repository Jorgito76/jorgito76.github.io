White Space

Facebook

Visit Facebook

White space on a page entails confidence. The login page for Facebook is a great example of white space. It is used to get the reader focused on the credentials needed to login or if they want to create an account and the simple Facebook logo while allowing areas that don't include any content.

Visual Hierarchy

Reddit

Visit Reddit

Visual Hierarchy is about putting the most important thing first for the reader to start at. It gives direction, sense of topic and pushes the reader to scroll endlessly. Reddit is a page that is about putting the most upvoted image, news, etc. upfront for the reader. This can get you to keep reading and learn what the page is about.

Contrast

Twitter

Visit Twitter

Contrast helps the eye of the reader feel welcomed and invites further reading. Even though we have one color, the shade and tint magnify the login Page and becomes pleasing to the eye to follow.
